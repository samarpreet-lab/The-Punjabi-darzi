Fabric Care Instructions

Keep your Punjabi suits looking beautiful

Washing

Always check fabric care labels. Use cold water for delicate fabrics.

Drying

Avoid direct sunlight. Dry in shade to prevent color fading.

Ironing

Use appropriate heat settings. Iron silk on low heat with a cloth.

Storage

Store in breathable bags. Use cedar balls to prevent moths.

How to Choose the Right Fabric

Consider the Occasion

For daily wear, choose breathable cotton. For weddings and festivals, opt for silk or designer fabrics with embellishments.

Think About the Season

Cotton and georgette are perfect for summer, while silk and heavier fabrics work well in winter and monsoon.

Color Considerations

Light colors are ideal for day events and summer, while rich, darker tones are perfect for evening occasions.

Budget Planning

Set a realistic budget including fabric, stitching, and embellishment costs. Quality fabric is an investment.
